========

CSS Text Properties:
====================

1) font-size – Sets how big or small the text appears.

2) font-weight – Controls how bold or light the text looks.

3) font-family – Chooses the font style used for the text.

4) line-height – Adjusts the space between lines of text.

5) margin – Adds space outside the text block or element.

6) text-decoration: underline; – Underlines the text.

7) text-align: center; – Aligns the text to the center.



HTML TEXT TAGS:
===============

1) <span></span> – Styles only the selected part of the text inline.

2) <u></u> – Underlines only the text inside the tag.

3) <strong></strong> – Makes only the selected text bold
